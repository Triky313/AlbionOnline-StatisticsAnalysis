Albion Online Statistics Analysis

A tool for easily reading auction house data, with a loot logger, damage meter, dungeon tracker, dungeon entry timer, crafting calculator, map history, and player information.

Designed and developed by Triky313.

Statistics Analysis Tool - Download
  • You need Windows 10 or higher.
  • Install the .NET 10.0 Desktop Runtime v10.0.0 or higher here to start the tool.
  • Tracking via Socket: If socket tracking is used, the tool only needs to be started as an administrator to work properly.
  • Tracking via Npcap: As an alternative, you can use the Npcap version. For this, Npcap must be installed.
    Additional filtering is available for VPN users, allowing IP and port filtering. Download Npcap

Features

Header

Next to the tool name, the selected server is displayed, along with whether tracking is fully active.

Additional information about your current character is also displayed.

Below that, your player character and, if available, your party members are listed. The small icon next to the player count can be clicked to copy a comma-separated list of all party members to your clipboard.

On the right side, you can see how long tracking has been running.



Item Search

The item search displays all items in the game. Double-click an item to open its window and view more information.



Logging

The Logging tab displays items collected by nearby players. Fame, Silver, and Faction Points can also be displayed here.



Dungeons

The Dungeons tab displays Solo Dungeons, Group Dungeons, Avalonian Dungeons, Mists, Mist Dungeons, Hellgates, Corrupted Dungeons, and HCEs with detailed information.



Damage Meter

The Damage Meter shows the damage and healing done by you and the players in your party. You can also create snapshots of your damage meter so you do not lose them.



Trade Monitoring

The Trade Monitoring tab shows sold and purchased items that you received by in-game mail or traded directly on the market. It also displays trade statistics. You can also create manual entries.



Gathering

The Gathering tab shows information about your gathered resources. Fishing is also displayed.



Party Builder

The Party Builder shows the item power of your character and your party members. Party member items can be updated when you retrieve the player's values.



Storage History

When you open chests in the bank, in a hideout, or on your islands, the contents present at that time are saved and displayed in the history.



Map History

The Map History tab shows the maps you have entered, sorted from newest to oldest.



Open World

This tab tracks mobs and how often they have been killed.



Player Information

Player information can be loaded from the Albion Online servers here.



The features mentioned above are the most important ones, but there are many more waiting to be tried out.
