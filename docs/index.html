Albion Online Statistics Analysis

A tool to easily read auction house data with a loot logger, damage meter, dungeon tracker, dungeon entry timer, crafting calculator, map history and player information

Designed and programmed by Triky313

Statistics Analysis Tool - Download
  • You need Windows 10 or higher
  • Install .NET 7.0 Desktop Runtime (v7.0.9 or higher) here (To start the tool)

Features

Header

Next to the name of the tool, the selected server is displayed and whether the tool is completely active in tracking or not.

Additionally, there is information about the character you are playing with.

Below that the player himself and if available the party members are listed. The small icon next to the number of players can be clicked and you get a coma separated list of all players in the party in your clipboard.

On the right side you can see how long the tracking has been running.



Item search

In the item search all items of the game are displayed. With a double click you can open the window of the item and get more information.



Logging

In the logging tab, items collected by nearby players can be displayed. Fame, Silver and Faction Points can also be displayed here.



Dungeons

The Dungeon Tab displays Solo Dungeons, Group Dungeons, Avas, Mists, Mist Dungeons, Hellgates, Corrupted Dungeons and HCEs with lots of information.



Damage Meter

The Damage Meter shows the damage and healing of you and players in your party. You can also take snapshots of your damage meter so you don't lose them.



Trade Monitoring

The Trade Monitoring tab shows you sold and bought items that you have received by mail in the game or traded directly in the market. Additionally, statistics about the trades are displayed. You can also create manual entries.



Gathering

The Gathering Tab shows you information about your gathered resources. Fishing is also displayed.



Party Builder

The Party Builder shows you the IP of your character and your party members. Items of party members can be updated when you call the values of the player.



Storage History

When you open the chests in the bank, in the hideout or on your islands, the contents that were present at that time are saved and displayed in the history.



Map History

In the Map History you can see the maps you have entered. Sorted from top to bottom they are displayed.



Player Information

Here player information can be loaded from the Albion Online servers.



The features mentioned above are the most important ones, but there are a lot of other features just waiting to be tried out.
